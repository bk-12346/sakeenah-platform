Focused 12-week roadmap

1

Trust-copy rewrite

Rewrite onboarding around emotional promise, one intentional reflection per day, and accurate privacy language.

2

Completion UX polish

Make the fourth-turn ending feel intentional. Guide users toward their journal and tomorrow's reflection.

3

Hardening pass

Add journal error handling, responsive browser checks, RLS regression checks, and deterministic crisis screening.

4

Retention baseline

Track Day-1 and Day-7 return behavior, completed reflections, signup conversion, and common emotional categories.

5

Memory v1

Pass the authenticated user's last one to three reflections into the initial response context. Avoid embeddings and vector databases.

6

Follow-up quality

Use feedback to improve short faith-native follow-up questions and remove generic wellness suggestions.

7

Journal journey redesign

Make journal cards easier to scan and let users expand the full reflection thread.

8

User interviews

Talk to 10-15 users. Identify what felt meaningful, generic, confusing, or worth returning for.

9

Ask Sakeenah

Add simple questions over recent journal history to create a useful feeling of continuity.

10

Weekly reflection summary

Generate a gentle weekly mirror for users with at least two reflections that week.

11

Soft paywall copy

Test a premium explanation around memory, weekly summaries, Ask Sakeenah, and archive access without integrating payments yet.

12

Metrics review

Review retention and qualitative feedback. Decide whether payments or further core-loop improvements are justified.

Defer until retention proves the need

Later
  • Native mobile app and PWA installation
  • Anthropic migration
  • Community features
  • Audio responses and voice input
  • Prayer-time notifications
  • Therapist directory or marketplace
  • Payment integration and large content libraries
